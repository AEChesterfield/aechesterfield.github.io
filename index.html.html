Profile

Commercial depth.
Creative instinct.

I am a senior marketing and commercial leader with fifteen-plus years of progressive experience across brand management, private label product development, integrated campaign execution and go-to-market strategy — built across consumer health, FMCG, personal care and lifestyle brand environments in ANZ, APAC and EMEA.

My career has been defined by the ability to operate across disciplines — from brand strategy and campaign management to TGA-regulated product development, OEM sourcing and private label origination. I have pioneered private label strategies, led NPD pipelines end-to-end, and built brand equity for globally recognised marks across some of the most competitive retail categories in Australia and internationally.

I bring particular depth in regulated consumer health and personal care — nine-plus years developing and marketing topical products within the TGA framework, including formulations incorporating Hyaluronic Acid, L-Arginine and other regulated actives — alongside deep channel expertise across Australian pharmacy and grocery retail.
